Group TriViz — Somayyeh Eslami • Moein Vadikhayr • Elaheh Sheidaei

This website is built on the Wars by Location and Purpose (1816–2001) dataset, which documents 464 major wars across the globe. Unlike traditional war datasets, it identifies both the geographic location and the purpose of each conflict, covering wars fought by states, pre-independent kingdoms, and empires.
