Section Title

Wars by Location and Purpose

Short summary: This project analyzes global war patterns by geography and purpose, building visual narratives from data.

Introduction

This section introduces what you’ll find in the project: datasets used, comparative categories, and the story behind the visuals. It contains five core visualizations, each addressing different research questions.

What to Expect

  • Comparison of wars by continent or region
  • Differences in war purposes across regions
  • Temporal patterns before and after major world events

Research Questions

  1. How many wars occurred in each region, and how do purposes vary across them?
  2. How has the number of wars changed over time for each type or purpose?
  3. Which types of wars tend to last longer on average?
